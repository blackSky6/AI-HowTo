## TL;DR

**Canva AI** is the best free option — easy, customizable, and you keep full rights. For a more polished brand identity with variations, **Looka** is worth the $20 one-time fee.

## Top Pick: Canva AI

Canva's built-in AI logo maker lets you describe your brand and generates multiple logo options instantly. You can customize fonts, colors, and layouts without any design skills.

- **Best for:** Small businesses, side projects, personal brands
- **Price:** Free (Canva Pro $13/mo unlocks more assets)
- **Why we love it:** Instantly editable, exports as SVG, no designer needed

## Step-by-Step: Making a Logo with Canva AI

### Step 1: Go to Canva's Logo Maker
Visit [canva.com](https://canva.com) → Search "logo maker" → Click "AI Logo Generator"

### Step 2: Describe Your Brand
Enter:
- Your business name
- Industry/category (tech, food, fitness, etc.)
- Style preferences (minimal, bold, playful, professional)

> **Example:** "TechFlow — a productivity software company. Style: minimal, modern, blue and white."

### Step 3: Pick a Starting Design
Canva generates 8-12 logo options. Choose the one closest to what you want — you'll customize it next.

### Step 4: Customize
- Change fonts (hundreds of options)
- Adjust colors (use your brand hex code)
- Swap icons or remove them
- Resize proportions

### Step 5: Download
- Free: PNG with white background
- Pro: SVG vector (resizes without blur), transparent PNG

## Alternative: Looka for Full Brand Kits

Worth it if you need consistent branding across multiple materials.

## FAQ

**Can I trademark an AI-generated logo?**
Yes, if you've customized it significantly. A logo generated and downloaded as-is without modification may not be trademarkable. Always consult a trademark attorney for important brands.

**Is Canva's AI logo free?**
The basic generator is free. SVG/vector exports and premium icon packs require Canva Pro ($13/mo).

**What file format do I need for printing?**
Always get SVG or PDF for print. PNG works for digital use but gets blurry when enlarged.
