## Step-by-Step: Making a Presentation with Gamma

### Step 1: Go to Gamma
Visit [gamma.app](https://gamma.app) and sign up free.

### Step 2: Create from Prompt
Click "Create New" → "Generate with AI"

Enter a specific prompt:
> "Create a 10-slide pitch deck for a B2B SaaS startup that helps restaurants reduce food waste using AI forecasting. Include problem, solution, market size, product, business model, traction, team, and ask."

The more specific you are, the better the output.

### Step 3: Review the AI Outline
Gamma shows you an outline first (slide titles and key points). Edit anything before it generates the full deck.

### Step 4: Generate
Click "Generate" — 30-60 seconds later, you have a full designed deck.

### Step 5: Edit and Customize
- Click any slide to edit text
- Change the theme (color palette, fonts)
- Swap images (Gamma has a built-in Unsplash integration)
- Add charts, embed YouTube videos, or include live data

### Step 6: Share or Export
- Share via link (best for online presentations)
- Export to PowerPoint (.pptx)
- Export to PDF

## Using ChatGPT + PowerPoint Together

If you prefer PowerPoint but want AI help:

1. Ask ChatGPT: "Create an outline for a 10-slide presentation about [topic]. For each slide, give me: title, 3 bullet points, and suggested visuals."
2. Copy the outline into PowerPoint manually
3. Use PowerPoint Designer (built-in AI) to auto-format layouts
4. Or paste ChatGPT's content into a Canva template

## FAQ

**Can I export Gamma slides to PowerPoint?**
Yes. Gamma Pro exports to .pptx. Free tier exports to PDF only.

**Will the AI presentation look generic?**
By default, yes — but Gamma's designs are better than most generic templates. Customize the color theme and swap the photos for a unique look.

**What if I need speaker notes?**
Ask ChatGPT: "Write speaker notes for each of these slides [paste slide content]." Then paste into your deck.
