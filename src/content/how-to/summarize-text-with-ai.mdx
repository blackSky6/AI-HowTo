## TL;DR

**ChatGPT** handles most summarization tasks well. For long PDFs and research papers, **NotebookLM** is free and excellent. For web articles with citations, **Perplexity** is the fastest option.

## Top Pick: Claude (for Long Documents)

Claude has the largest context window of any mainstream AI — up to 200K tokens. That means it can read an entire book and summarize it in one go.

- **Best for:** Long documents, research papers, legal contracts
- **Price:** Free / $20/mo Pro
- **Why we love it:** Handles massive documents without truncating

## How to Summarize Different Content Types

### Summarizing an Article (URL)

**With Perplexity (Free):**
1. Go to [perplexity.ai](https://perplexity.ai)
2. Paste the URL and ask: "Summarize this article in 5 bullet points"
3. Perplexity fetches the page and responds with citations

**With ChatGPT:**
1. Copy the full article text
2. Paste into ChatGPT
3. Prompt: "Summarize this in 3 paragraphs, focus on the key findings"

### Summarizing a PDF

**With NotebookLM (Free, Best Option):**
1. Go to [notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com)
2. Create a new notebook → Upload your PDF
3. Ask any question about the document, or click "Notebook Guide" for an auto-summary
4. NotebookLM cites exact page numbers in responses

**With Claude:**
1. Drag and drop the PDF directly into the chat
2. Ask: "Give me a 1-page executive summary of this document"

### Summarizing a YouTube Video

1. Go to [YouTube.com](https://youtube.com) and open the video
2. Below the video, click "..." → "Show transcript"
3. Copy the transcript
4. Paste into ChatGPT with: "Summarize this transcript in 5 key takeaways"

Alternatively, use browser extensions like **Merlin** or **Glasp** that add one-click AI summaries to YouTube.

### Summarizing a Long Email Thread

In Gmail:
1. Click the "Summarize this email" button (appears on long threads in Gmail with Gemini)
2. Or copy the thread, paste into ChatGPT with: "What is this email thread about and what action is needed from me?"

## FAQ

**How accurate are AI summaries?**
Very accurate for factual summarization. AI doesn't fabricate content when summarizing real text — it just condenses it. Always skim the original for critical decisions.

**Can AI summarize in other languages?**
Yes. ChatGPT and Claude can summarize English documents and output the summary in Chinese, Spanish, French, etc. Just add "in [language]" to your prompt.

**What's the longest document AI can summarize?**
Claude Pro handles up to 200,000 tokens (~150,000 words — most books). NotebookLM handles up to 500,000 words per notebook.
