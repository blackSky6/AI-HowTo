## TL;DR

**Descript** is the most revolutionary AI video editor — edit video by editing the transcript, like a Word doc. **CapCut** (free) handles auto-captions and basic AI edits for social media. **Runway** is the best for advanced AI effects.

## Top Pick: Descript

Descript transcribes your video, then lets you edit by deleting text from the transcript. Delete the word "um" everywhere? One click. Cut a rambling section? Delete the sentences. The video updates automatically.

- **Best for:** Podcasters, YouTubers, talking-head videos
- **Price:** Free (1 hr/mo) / $12/mo Creator
- **Why we love it:** Makes video editing as easy as editing a Google Doc

## 5 AI Video Editing Tasks

### Task 1: Auto-Remove Filler Words

**With Descript:**
1. Upload your video → Descript transcribes automatically
2. Go to "Remove filler words"
3. Select: "um," "uh," "like," "you know"
4. Click "Remove All" → Descript cuts those moments from video

This single feature saves 30+ minutes on a typical talking-head video.

### Task 2: Auto-Captions

**With CapCut (Free):**
1. Import video into CapCut
2. Click "Text" → "Auto Captions"
3. Select language → AI transcribes and places captions
4. Edit any mistakes → Export

**Accuracy:** ~95% for clear speech in English.

### Task 3: AI Background Removal (for Talking Head Videos)

**With CapCut or Runway:**
1. Import clip where you're talking to camera
2. CapCut: Effects → Background Removal → Auto
3. Replace with any background image or video

No green screen needed.

### Task 4: AI B-Roll Generation

**With Runway Gen-3:**
1. Select a section of your timeline
2. Type a description of the visual you want
3. Runway generates matching video footage
4. Drag into your timeline as B-roll

Useful for filling gaps where you don't have footage.

### Task 5: Video Enhancement

**With Topaz Video AI:**
- Upscale old 720p footage to 4K
- Remove motion blur
- Reduce video noise from low-light shots

### Task 6: Auto-Edit for Social Media

**With CapCut AI "Smart Cut":**
1. Upload a long video (webinar, podcast, interview)
2. CapCut identifies the best 60-second highlights
3. Exports multiple short-form clips for TikTok/Reels/Shorts

## FAQ

**Can AI edit a full YouTube video automatically?**
Partially. AI can handle captions, filler word removal, and highlight selection. Creative cuts, pacing, and storytelling still benefit from human judgment.

**Is CapCut free for commercial use?**
CapCut is free for personal and commercial use. Some AI features require a Pro subscription ($8/mo). Check their licensing for content used in paid ads.

**Will AI video editing replace video editors?**
For simple content (social clips, talking heads), AI handles 80% of the work. Complex narrative editing, color grading, and motion graphics remain human-dominated for now.
