## TL;DR

For the best image quality, **Midjourney** is still the gold standard. For free and unlimited generation, try **Stable Diffusion** locally. If you're already in Adobe, use **Firefly** — it's built right into Photoshop.

## Top Pick: Midjourney

Midjourney consistently produces the most visually stunning results. It runs through Discord, which feels odd at first but works great once you get used to it.

- **Best for:** High-quality artistic images
- **Price:** $10/mo (Basic), $30/mo (Standard)
- **Why we love it:** Photorealistic outputs, incredible artistic range

## Step-by-Step: Generating Your First Image

### Step 1: Choose Your Tool
Pick based on your needs:
- **Midjourney** — best quality, paid
- **DALL-E 3 (ChatGPT)** — easy, good for ChatGPT Plus users
- **Adobe Firefly** — best for commercial-safe content
- **Stable Diffusion** — free, runs locally, steep learning curve

### Step 2: Write a Good Prompt
Prompt quality determines output quality. A strong prompt includes:
- **Subject:** What you want (e.g., "a red fox in a forest")
- **Style:** Photorealistic, cartoon, oil painting, etc.
- **Lighting:** Golden hour, studio light, dramatic shadows
- **Camera/Lens:** (optional) 85mm portrait, wide angle

> **Good prompt:** "A red fox sitting in a misty forest at golden hour, photorealistic, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field"
>
> **Bad prompt:** "fox in woods"

### Step 3: Iterate and Refine
- Use `--v 6` in Midjourney for the latest model
- Click **V1–V4** to create variations
- Click **U1–U4** to upscale your favorite
- Keep tweaking your prompt until you get what you want

### Step 4: Download and Use
- Right-click to save in Discord (Midjourney)
- Check licensing — Midjourney Pro allows commercial use

## FAQ

**Can I use AI-generated images commercially?**
It depends on the tool. Adobe Firefly and DALL-E 3 are generally safe for commercial use. Midjourney requires a Pro plan ($60/mo) for commercial rights.

**What's the best free AI image generator?**
Adobe Firefly offers 25 free credits/month. For unlimited free generation, Stable Diffusion running locally is your best bet.

**How do I make AI images look more realistic?**
Add photorealistic descriptors to your prompt: "shot on Canon 5D, 8K, hyperrealistic, professional photography."
