## Step-by-Step: Creating a Song with Suno

### Step 1: Go to Suno
Visit [suno.com](https://suno.com) and sign in with Google or Discord.

### Step 2: Describe Your Song
In the prompt box, describe:
- Genre (pop, rock, jazz, lo-fi, hip-hop, etc.)
- Mood (upbeat, melancholic, energetic, chill)
- Topic/theme (summer vacation, heartbreak, motivation)
- Optional: tempo, instruments, era (80s synth-pop, etc.)

> **Example prompt:** "Upbeat indie pop song about starting a new adventure, acoustic guitar, optimistic tone, catchy chorus"

### Step 3: Use Custom Mode for More Control
Click "Custom" to:
- Write your own lyrics
- Choose the style more precisely
- Set the structure (verse, chorus, bridge)

### Step 4: Generate and Iterate
- Click "Create" — two variations generate simultaneously
- Listen to both, pick your favorite
- Click "Extend" to make it longer
- Click "Create similar" to get variations on the same vibe

### Step 5: Download
- Free: MP3 download with Suno watermark
- Pro: Full quality WAV, no watermark, commercial license ($8/mo)

## FAQ

**Is AI-generated music royalty-free?**
On paid plans (Suno Pro, Udio Standard), yes — you own commercial rights. Free tiers typically allow personal use only.

**Can AI music sound professional?**
Current AI music is great for demos, backgrounds, and casual use. For professional releases, it still needs human refinement, but the gap is closing fast.

**What genres does AI music handle best?**
Pop, lo-fi, and electronic styles work best. Orchestral and jazz are improving but can still sound slightly "off."
