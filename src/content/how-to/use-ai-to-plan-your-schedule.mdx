## TL;DR

**Motion** is the best AI scheduling tool — it automatically arranges your tasks around your calendar based on priority and deadlines. For free alternatives, a well-crafted ChatGPT prompt works surprisingly well for weekly planning.

## Top Pick: Motion

Motion is an AI calendar that auto-schedules your task list around your existing meetings. You add tasks with due dates and priorities; Motion finds the best time slot automatically.

- **Best for:** Knowledge workers, managers, anyone with 20+ tasks/week
- **Price:** $19/mo (worth it if you're always behind)
- **Why we love it:** Adapts in real-time when meetings change or tasks run over

## Method 1: AI-Powered Calendar (Motion/Reclaim.ai)

### Using Reclaim.ai (Free tier available)
1. Sign up at [reclaim.ai](https://reclaim.ai)
2. Connect Google Calendar
3. Add your habits (gym, focus blocks, lunch)
4. Add tasks with priorities and time estimates
5. Reclaim automatically finds open slots and protects them

Free tier: 3 habits, 3 task integrations. Paid ($8/mo) unlocks full power.

### Using Motion ($19/mo)
1. Connect Google/Outlook calendar
2. Add all open tasks with:
   - Priority (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
   - Time estimate
   - Due date
3. Motion auto-schedules everything
4. When a meeting is added, Motion automatically reschedules displaced tasks

## Method 2: ChatGPT as a Planning Assistant

You don't need a paid app. ChatGPT is surprisingly effective as a planning partner.

**The Weekly Planning Prompt:**
```
I need help planning my week. Here's my situation:
- Available hours: Mon-Fri 9am-6pm (1hr lunch)
- Recurring commitments: [list your meetings]
- This week's tasks: [paste your task list]
- Deadlines: [list any deadlines]
- Personal priorities: [what must get done this week]

Please create a day-by-day schedule with specific time blocks. Group similar tasks. Put my most important work in my best hours (mornings for me). Leave buffer time.
```

**The Daily Planning Prompt:**
```
It's Monday morning. I have 6 hours of focused work time today. Here are my tasks: [list]. Which 3 should I focus on today and in what order? Explain your reasoning.
```

## Method 3: Notion AI for Task Prioritization

If you manage tasks in Notion:
1. Open your task database
2. Click "AI" in any empty block
3. Ask: "Prioritize these tasks by urgency and importance (Eisenhower matrix)"
4. Notion AI reads your database and creates a prioritized list

## Building a Better Schedule (AI or Not)

When telling AI how to plan your day, provide:
- **Energy patterns:** "I'm sharpest 9-11am, foggy after lunch"
- **Context switching cost:** "I need 30min to get into deep work"
- **Constraints:** "School pickup at 3:30pm"
- **Work style:** "I prefer finishing one thing completely vs. parallel tasks"

The more context you give, the better the plan.

## FAQ

**Is Motion worth $19/month?**
If you routinely miss deadlines or feel overwhelmed by tasks, yes. If your schedule is simple or you have fewer than 10 active tasks, start with Reclaim's free tier.

**Can AI scheduling tools handle recurring tasks?**
Yes — Motion and Reclaim both support recurring tasks (daily standup, weekly review, etc.) and will schedule them automatically.

**What if my priorities change mid-week?**
Motion and Reclaim both adapt in real-time. Change a due date or priority and the whole week reschedules automatically.
