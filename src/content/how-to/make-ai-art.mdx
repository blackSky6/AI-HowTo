## TL;DR

AI art has never been more accessible. **Midjourney** makes the most beautiful images. **DALL-E 3** (via ChatGPT) is easiest to use. **Stable Diffusion** is free if you're willing to run it locally. Start with whatever fits your budget.

## What Is AI Art?

AI art is any image created by an AI model based on a text description (called a "prompt"). You describe what you want in plain English, and the AI generates it in seconds.

No drawing skills required. None.

## Step-by-Step: Creating AI Art with Midjourney

Midjourney consistently produces the most visually stunning results.

### Step 1: Join the Discord
1. Go to [midjourney.com](https://midjourney.com)
2. Click "Join the Beta" → Join Midjourney Discord
3. Subscribe to a plan ($10/mo Basic)

### Step 2: Go to a Newbies Channel
In the Discord sidebar, find any `#newbies-` channel. Type your command there.

### Step 3: Use the /imagine Command
Type: `/imagine` then press Tab to activate the prompt field.

Enter your description:
> `/imagine a glowing forest at night, bioluminescent plants, watercolor style, ethereal atmosphere`

Press Enter — Midjourney generates 4 image variations in ~60 seconds.

### Step 4: The Art of Prompting

Good prompts follow this structure:
**[Subject] + [Setting] + [Style] + [Lighting] + [Details]**

| Component | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| Subject | A samurai warrior |
| Setting | Standing in a cherry blossom storm |
| Style | Digital painting, studio ghibli-inspired |
| Lighting | Dramatic backlight, golden hour |
| Details | Intricate armor, flowing robes |

**Full prompt:** "A samurai warrior standing in a cherry blossom storm, digital painting, studio ghibli-inspired, dramatic backlight, golden hour, intricate armor, flowing robes"

### Step 5: Refine Your Favorites
- **U1-U4:** Upscale (get a high-res version of that image)
- **V1-V4:** Variation (make similar images with small differences)
- **🔄 Redo:** Regenerate entirely
- Add `--v 6.1` for the latest model
- Add `--ar 16:9` for widescreen, `--ar 1:1` for square

## FAQ

**Do I own the AI art I create?**
Policies vary. Adobe Firefly = yes. Midjourney Pro = yes (commercial). DALL-E 3 = yes (OpenAI terms allow commercial use). Always check the tool's terms before selling AI art.

**Why does AI art look weird sometimes?**
AI struggles with hands, text, and complex symmetry. Use `--no hands` or zoom out to avoid these problem areas.

**Can I sell AI-generated art?**
Yes, on most platforms with a paid subscription. Many artists sell AI art on Etsy, Redbubble, and as NFTs.
