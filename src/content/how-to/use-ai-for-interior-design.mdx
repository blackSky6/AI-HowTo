## Step-by-Step: Redesign a Room with RoomGPT

### Step 1: Take a Good Photo
The better your input photo, the better the output:
- Natural daylight works best
- Capture the full room, not just a corner
- Keep the camera level (no tilted shots)
- Clear the clutter — AI will redesign furniture, not junk

### Step 2: Go to RoomGPT
Visit [roomgpt.io](https://roomgpt.io) — no sign-up required for basic use.

### Step 3: Upload Your Room
Click "Upload Photo" and select your room image.

### Step 4: Choose a Room Type and Style
**Room type:** Living room, bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, office, dining room

**Design styles:**
- Modern
- Minimalist
- Professional (office)
- Tropical
- Coastal
- Vintage
- Industrial
- Scandinavian
- Bohemian

### Step 5: Generate
Click "Generate Dream Room" — takes 10-30 seconds.

You get one result on free tier. Generate multiple for comparison on Pro.

### Step 6: Download and Compare
Save your favorite results. Share with family or a contractor for real renovation planning.

## Method 2: More Control with Midjourney

For highly customized results:

1. Take your room photo
2. In Midjourney, use: `/imagine [upload photo] redesign this room in Japandi style, minimalist furniture, neutral tones, warm lighting --v 6`
3. Iterate with more specific prompts

This takes more effort but gives more creative control.

## FAQ

**Can AI interior design tools suggest specific furniture to buy?**
Most just show visuals. Some newer tools (like Modsy, now part of other platforms) link to purchasable items. Otherwise, take the AI render to a furniture store and match it yourself.

**Is AI interior design accurate enough to plan real renovations?**
For concept and style direction, yes. For actual measurements and technical planning, always work with a licensed designer or contractor.

**Can it work for commercial spaces like offices?**
Yes. RoomGPT has an "Office" room type. For corporate spaces, Spacely AI and custom Midjourney workflows give more professional results.
