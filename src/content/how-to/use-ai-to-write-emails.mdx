## TL;DR

If you spend more than 10 minutes a day writing emails, AI can cut that in half. **ChatGPT** is great for general drafting, but if you want something built directly into your inbox, **Grammarly** or native workspace AI (like Google Workspace or Copilot) is the way to go.

## Top Pick: Grammarly

Grammarly isn't just a spellchecker anymore. Its generative AI understands your tone and helps you draft replies directly in Gmail or Outlook.

- **Best for:** Seamless inbox integration
- **Price:** Free tier available ($12/mo for Premium)
- **Why we love it:** You don't have to copy-paste between tabs.

## Step-by-Step Guide: Drafting an Email with ChatGPT

If you want the most control over the output, using a chatbot like ChatGPT or Claude is the best approach.

### Step 1: Provide Context
Don't just say "write an email to my boss." Tell the AI:
- Who you are emailing (Boss, client, team)
- What the goal is (Ask for time off, pitch an idea)
- What tone to use (Professional, friendly, urgent)

> **Prompt Example:** "Write an email to my manager asking for next Friday off for a family event. Keep the tone professional but warm. Mention that my projects are on track."

### Step 2: Refine the Output
AI rarely gets it perfect on the first try. Ask it to adjust:
- "Make it shorter."
- "Sound more confident."
- "Add a bulleted list of my current projects."

### Step 3: Review and Send
**Never send an AI-generated email without reading it first.** Check for strange phrasing, overly formal language (AI loves saying "I hope this email finds you well"), or factual errors.

## Comparing Email AI Tools

| Tool | Best For | Price | Pros | Cons |
|------|----------|-------|------|------|
| ChatGPT | Drafting from scratch | Free / $20/mo | Highly versatile, understands context | Requires copy-pasting |
| Grammarly | Inbox integration | Free / $12/mo | Works inside email clients, checks tone | Free version has limits |
| Claude | Long, complex emails | Free / $20/mo | Very natural tone, less "robotic" | No native email integration |
